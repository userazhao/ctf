Felix the Helix says:
Ever wonder how our bodies create proteins? Each of our cells contain little pieces of biological machinery called ribosomes. These ribosomes decode long strings of RNA into long strings of amino acids, with each amino acid represented by a codon (sequence of 3 bases). These strings of amino acids are what we call proteins.
